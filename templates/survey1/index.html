This is a survey about WiFi provision within the Blizard Building at Whitechapel towards the end of 2009. It is intended to guage of the current provision before trials start of some new systems during the first part of 2010. There will be other surveys towards the end of each trial to help us assess the quality of each system tried, and help us select a system to replace the current one. You can answer this survey more than once if, for example, you have rather different experiences in different parts of the building, or using different devices.
