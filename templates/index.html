IT Services are planning to upgrade the WiFi system at Whitechapel. The WiFi systems available have been evaluated and there are a small number of competing systems on a "short list". To complete the work of choosing the most suitable system some trials will be conducted at Whitechapel in the Blizard Building; chosen because it presents a "challenging" environment for radio signals. We are asking people who use the building to complete some surveys to help us assess the performance of the competing systems. The first survey is to assess the state of the old system to help assess how much the new systems improve on the old one as each is trialed. The second survey is to evaluate the first set of trial equipment; it essentially asks the same as the first survey with a couple of extra questions asking directly if you have noticed any change in the quality of the service.
